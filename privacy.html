Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Jul 13, 2025

Welcome to Splituh, a group finance management application designed to simplify expense tracking, bill splitting, and financial collaboration among friends, families, and teams. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and third-party login details.
  • Transaction Data: Details of expenses, group activity, and settlements.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Splituh app and its features.
  • Facilitate group expense tracking and settlements.
  • Communicate with you about updates, promotions, and support.

3. Data Sharing and Security

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We may share data with trusted service providers to enhance our services.
  • We implement strong security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of promotional communications.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

5. Compliance with Laws

Splituh complies with applicable data protection laws, including:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000
  • IT Rules, 2021
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA)
  • GDPR, if applicable to EU users

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
